# DMARC Email Security Services for Small Businesses

**Professional DMARC, SPF, DKIM configuration and monitoring**  
For small businesses, freelancers, and agencies that rely on email for brand reputation, customer trust, and deliverability. Another potential market would be contracting with MSPs that offer DMARC services.

---

## What You Get

**Initial Configuration**  
- Create or correct SPF, DKIM, and DMARC DNS records  
- Align sending sources to achieve DMARC compliance  
- Point DMARC RUA and RUF reports to monitoring platform

**Monitoring & Reporting**  
- Access to a dashboard for ongoing compliance visibility  
- Weekly status summaries (optional)  
- Alerting for non-compliant senders or spoofing attempts

**Ongoing Support**  
- Help identifying and authenticating 3rd-party senders (e.g., Salesforce, Mailchimp, HR platforms)  
- Advise on policy transitions (from `p=none` to `quarantine`/`reject`)  
- Deliverability troubleshooting guidance

---

## Why This Matters

Even small businesses face email spoofing.  
Without DMARC enforcement, anyone can send email *as you*.  
Misconfigured SPF or DKIM can silently block your real mail from reaching inboxes.  
You deserve to look legitimate — and land in the inbox — every time.
